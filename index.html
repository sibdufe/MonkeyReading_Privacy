Privacy Policy for MonkeyReading

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Introduction

MonkeyReading ("we," "our," or "us") is a digital storybook application designed for families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

IMPORTANT:

MonkeyReading is intended for use by parents and guardians to create content for children. Parents maintain full control over the app through Admin Mode.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email Address: Required for account creation, authentication, and important notifications
  • Password: Securely hashed and stored through Firebase Authentication

2. User-Created Content

  • Photos: Images you add to your storybooks
  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings you create for storytelling
  • Story Books: Book titles, page names, and organizational data
  • All content is stored locally on your device and optionally backed up to secure cloud storage if you choose

3. Device Information

  • Device Identifiers: Hardware-based device fingerprints to manage the 2-device limit for account security
  • Device Metadata: Device name, platform (iOS/Android), OS version, app version
  • IP Address: Stored temporarily for security and fraud prevention

4. Usage Information

  • Storage Usage: Amount of cloud storage used (for premium subscribers)
  • Subscription Status: Active, trial, or free tier status
  • Restore Activity: Timestamps of cloud backup restore operations

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

1. App Functionality

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Storing and syncing your storybooks across devices
  • Managing subscription and premium features
  • Enforcing device limits for account security

2. Service Improvement

  • Monitoring storage usage to prevent abuse
  • Tracking restore operations to enforce fair-use limits
  • Processing user feedback (if you choose to submit it)

3. Security & Fraud Prevention

  • Device fingerprinting to prevent unauthorized account access
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

  • ✓ Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted to/from our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • ✓ Encryption at Rest: All data stored in Firebase is encrypted
  • ✓ Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard methods
  • ✓ Access Controls: Strict database security rules limit data access

Data Storage Locations

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Firebase (Google Cloud Platform), hosted in nam5 (US region)
  • Local Storage: Your device's secure storage
  • Backups: Optional cloud backups in Firebase Storage

Data Sharing

WE DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We only share data with:

  1. Firebase/Google Cloud Platform: Our infrastructure provider that stores and processes your data under strict contractual obligations
  2. Google Play/Apple App Store: For subscription payment processing (we do NOT receive your payment card information)

Your Rights and Controls

Parental Control

Parents have full control over all content and data through Admin Mode, including:

  • Adding/removing photos and audio
  • Deleting books and pages
  • Managing cloud backups
  • Controlling subscription settings

Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your data at any time:

  1. Soft Delete: Books can be moved to "Deleted Books" and restored within the retention period
  2. Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your entire account and all associated data:
    • Go to Settings → Delete Account
    • All your books, photos, audio, and cloud backups will be permanently deleted
    • This action cannot be undone

Data Portability

Premium users can export their storybooks to device storage at any time.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

MonkeyReading is designed as a parent-controlled app for creating content for children.

Our Commitment

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13
  • All accounts must be created and managed by parents/guardians (18+)
  • Parents control all content creation and data
  • We do NOT display ads to children
  • We do NOT use data for behavioral advertising

Parental Consent

By creating an account, you represent that you are at least 18 years old and consent to using MonkeyReading to create content for your children.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Books: Retained for 30 days before permanent deletion (free users: max 2 books)
  • Expired Subscriptions: Grace period data scheduled for deletion (with advance notice)
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days

Subscription and Payments

  • Payment processing is handled by Google Play or Apple App Store
  • We do NOT receive or store your credit card information
  • We only receive confirmation of active subscription status
  • Subscriptions can be canceled through your app store account

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Displaying a notice in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date above

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
